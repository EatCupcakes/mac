About Us

I started Eat' Cupcakes in 2011 as a personal blog and an easy way to share my baking recipes with my friends and family. At the time, I felt a little lost in both my career and life, but the kitchen always kept me centered. My small hobby blog quickly ignited a passion for food photography and teaching others how to bake, not just what to bake. Just a year and a half after launching my blog, I quit my full-time job to build Eat' Cupcakes into a business...
